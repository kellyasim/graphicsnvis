PROJECT DESCRIPTION 

We have decide to do a Dungeon / RPG style game. The goal of the game is to defeat the turtles. 

GAMEPLAY: 
Rules:
1) Touch Enemy it takes away life.
2) you can attack the opponent.
3) you can only move with in the confines of the map


Interactions:
1) 'w' to move up 
2) 'a' to move left 
3) 'd' to move right
4) 's' to move down
5) 'j' to attack
6) 'p' to pause

GUI Visual:
> 'p' for tutorial in top left corner
> player health points and lives
> number of enemies left 

Win:
To win you need to defeat all opponents on the screen.

Lose:
you run out of hp and lives.
