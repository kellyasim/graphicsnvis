Press "play" to load the game. Please be patient as assets may take a while to load.


Use: 
Pushing start on the main menu will take you to the character select screen.
Click on a sprite to select which character you want to play as, then click start to play.

'w' or "up arrow" to move up
'a' or "left arrow" to move left
's' or "down arrow" to move down
'd' or "right arrow" to move right

'j' to attack
'p' for tutorial/move keys and to pause/unpause the game
'r' to restart the game
'm' to mute sound
